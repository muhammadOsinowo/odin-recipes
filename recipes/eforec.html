How to make Efo riro

Ingridents

  • Oil
  • Boiled meat
  • Spinach
  • Blended Pepper
  • Fish
  • Turkey
    Steps
  1. Get all your ingredients together: The boiled Meat, Fish, Turkey, Chicken, or any other choice of protein.
  2. Heat up the Oil: I like to heat up my Oil a bit. I’m not a fan of bleaching palm Oil so I simply add the Onion once the Oil is hot and while the Onion is cooking in the Oil, the palm oil too is cooking. That does the trick for me.
  3. Fry the blended peppers: I wish the computer has a function to show emotions – emoticons are not strong enough to show the type of emphasis I’m talking about :). My emphasis is on fryyy… You need to fry till the water in the sauce evaporates and the sauce is reduced. Please remember to pay attention to the sauce, you don’t want it to get burnt, so be sure to stir constantly till you achieve that thick consistency.
  4. Season and add the Meat and Spinach: The sauce should be seasoned at this point with all those nice spices and condiments. Add the Meat, Stockfish, and dried Prawns or your choice of protein. Let it cook so that it can absorb all the yumminess of the sauce. Then, stir in the spinach.
  5. Finally: Adjust the seasoning if there’s a need to. Remove from heat and serve.
